#StopC23 Today

When every opposition party, from all ideologies, stands in unity... it's for a reason.

Who Are We?

We are a collaborative effort amongst opposition parties who currently do not have parliamentary representation. The parties which represent this united front of opposition to Bill C-23 (dubbed "Fair Elections Act") represent over 100,000 Canadians collectively.

What is Bill C-23?

Bill C-23 (the "Fair Elections Act") is a Conservative proposed legislation which fundamentally alters the Elections Act. We suggest reading Elections Canada's own interpretation of the law as it is written, here, for the basics of what the law will do.

Are We Alone?

In addition to our group of parties, composed entirely of parties who currently have no representation in parliament, every sitting party other than the Conservatives are opposed to the passing of C-23, Elections Canada has expressed concern over provisions of the act, Experts are concerned, and the vast majority of editorals for papers speak out against the act as well. The level of opposition to this single act is almost as unprecidented as the introduction of it in the first place.
